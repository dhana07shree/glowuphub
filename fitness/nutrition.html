A creamy, delicious smoothie packed with nutrients for radiant skin

  • 1 cup spinach (trust us, you won't taste it!)
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Ice cubes
Quick Steps:

  2. 2Add remaining ingredients
